How to scan your dependencies

If you’re unsure whether your project is secure, use DMX Scan to check for vulnerabilities in your dependency list.

01

Drag & drop or click the upload box to select your dependency file.

02

Select the dependency file type - Python(.txt) or npm(.json).

03

Click the ‘Scan’ button and wait a few moments for analysis.

04

Review the results to see which packages are vulnerable and safe.
